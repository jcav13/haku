about us

H A K U is a mobile massage therapy service that provides a personalised experience to each individual.

We aim to create a sense of relaxation, tranquility, and respect by adhering to the quality and precision of Japanese tradition.

Our founder, Momo-chan (ももちゃん), has several years of experience in the massage industry and a wealth of knowledge related to personalised care.

H A K U was created in the essence of Omotenashi (おもてなし). Omotenashi is the Japanese philosophy of wholehearted hospitality, where every detail is considered to ensure the utmost care and comfort for others. H A K U seeks to embody this same level of attentiveness and dedication, providing each and every individual with a truly thoughtful and refined experience.
